Albanian Regions

In this book is treated the geography and history of the Albanian regions in general and the geography of the Republic of Albania, Republic of Kosovo, Albanian regions in FYROM, Greece, Serbia, and Montenegro. The physical-geographical features, settlements, demography and economy are treated together with the natural and cultural heritage.

In addition, this book is enriched with statistical data from INSTAT of Albania and Kosovo. The data is official and belong to the latest census. The other data is taken from the districts, counties or is calculated. The book has about 35 pages with maps (25), images, photos and it is concluded with a dictionary for the technical and scientific terms.

The pre-historical settlements, antique cities, citadels, castles, and dwellings will show you the different architectural styles (Illyrian, Hellenic, Roman, Byzantine, Ottoman and modern).

The natural and cultural heritage constitute the foundation for the economical development of these regions. But the complicated history of the Albanian regions has made these regions to be broken up politically in six states and consequently having different economical and social development. Despite this fact, Albanian regions are unique concerning their ethno genesis, language, traditions and culture.

Reading this book you will understand that these regions are really blessed. Gorgeous, rich and impressive are the Albanian regions.
