About the Author

Dr. Merita Dollma studied geography at the University of Tirana and in 2004 she received the scientific grade of “Doctor of Sciences” for Environmental Management. Dr. Dollma has also studied at the Technical University of Dresden, University of Oxford, University of Cairo, University of Malta, etc. Currently, she is in United States performing scientific research at Eastern Michigan University as a Fulbright post doctorate scholar.

She has worked as a scientific researcher close to Geographic Studies Center of the Albanian Academy of Sciences from 1995-2007. Actually she is a lecturer at University of Tirana, Faculty of History-Filology, Department of Geography.

She is the author of text book for high schools, “Geography 3”, and co-author of the tourism guide book “Gate of the Orient”.
